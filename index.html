Understand products faster using customer reviews.

Our research tool analyzes e-commerce reviews and predicts whether a product is GOOD or BAD, then generates a short AI summary to help users decide quickly.

Web Extension GOOD / BAD Output AI Summary Review Collection
Download Extension How it Works See Demo

Features

Key capabilities designed for multilingual e-commerce review understanding.

Multilingual Detection

The system intelligently analyzes customer reviews written in Sinhala, English, and Singlish. This ensures accurate sentiment detection even when users mix languages.

Smart Product Insight

Quickly evaluates large numbers of reviews and provides a clear GOOD or BAD product prediction with an AI-generated summary for faster decisions.

User-Friendly Experience

Built for simplicity—one click to analyze. The interface is clean and easy to understand for both customers and sellers without technical knowledge.

How it Works

The extension collects reviews from the product page, processes the text, and displays the final prediction with an AI summary.

Step 1 — Open Product

Go to the product page on the e-commerce website.

Step 2 — Analyze

Click the “Analyze Product” button to start processing.

Step 3 — View Result

See GOOD/BAD prediction and AI-generated summary.
